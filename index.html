    Gramática del inglés organizada por nivel MCRL.

    A1Present simple, artículos definidos/indefinidos, pronombres.
    A2Past simple, present continuous, comparativos y adverbios de frecuencia.

    Sara visits a small museum in her city every Saturday. She reads the labels and listens to the guide. She likes to write a short note about her favorite object and the time of the visit.

      B) She reads labels.
      C) She buys books.
      D) She plays music.
    2. 2. How often does she visit the museum?
      A) Every day.
      B) Every Saturday.
      C) Once a year.
      D) Never.
    3. 3. What does she write?
      A) A shopping list.
      B) A note about her favorite object.
      C) A letter to a friend.
      D) A recipe.
    4. 4. What does she listen to?
      A) The radio.
      B) The museum guide.
      C) Music.
      D) The weather report.
    5. 5. Which word best describes the text?
      A) Difficult.
      B) Scientific.
      C) Simple.
      D) Fictional.
